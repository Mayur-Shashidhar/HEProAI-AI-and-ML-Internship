🔬 METHODOLOGY OVERVIEW
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

STEP 1: STUDENT NEEDS ANALYSIS
   → Calculate deficit scores: 100 - performance_score
   → Identify primary need: max(academic_need, wellness_need, career_need)

STEP 2: URGENCY ASSESSMENT
   → Evaluate 5 risk factors (GPA, attendance, completion, stress, cluster)
   → Assign urgency level: Critical/High/Medium/Low

STEP 3: MENTOR MATCHING
   → Filter mentors by type (academic/wellness/career)
   → Score based on availability, specialization, experience
   → Select best match

STEP 4: INTERVENTION GENERATION
   → Prescribe actions based on need × urgency matrix
   → Define meeting frequency and program duration
   → Create specific, actionable recommendations

STEP 5: ALERT SYSTEM
   → Generate alerts for Critical & High urgency students
   → Include mentor contact and immediate actions
   → Notify stakeholders for rapid response
